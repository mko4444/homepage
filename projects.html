Most of my time today is spent on a company I founded called Maisie. Maisie is a marketplace for group counseling sessions. The mission of the company is to bring affordable and accessible mental health care to everyone.

I publish a newsletter called The Paper, in which I reproduce very old newspaper articles around one topic each week. More info is located in the Newsletter section of this website.

I have worked a fair amount with a startup called Bloom Credit, whose mission is to democratize the US's credit system by using data to help consumers improve their credit scores.

I previously founded a financial technology company called Monet, which I stopped working on in my last year of college. Monet was a service that aggregated spending activity data across all of a customer's financial accounts and used machine learning, the internet, and other users to comb for suspicious activity.
