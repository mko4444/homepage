Each week, I publish a newsletter called The Paper, which takes one topic from history and examines it through the lens of that culture's newspapers or press.

You can sign up for this newsletter here. The newsletter is sent out each week on Sunday mornings. We also have Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram pages. If you enjoy The Paper and would like to support it, you can do so on Patreon.
